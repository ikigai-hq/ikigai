# Manage your students
> This section is used for student
> In this section, we will guide you how to add student and remove student of space

Ikigai space also manages your students, you can add or remove student in space setting.

## Add/Invite Student

You can invite students to join your Ikigai space by going to space [settings](./manage_your_space).
Click "Invite People," choose the expiration time, and click the "Generate" button.
The system will generate a new invite link, which your students can use to join by opening the link in their browser.

## Remove Student

If a student is not part of your class, you can remove them by going to space settings.
Click "Space Settings," then click the "Members" tab.
Find the student you want to remove and click "Remove."
