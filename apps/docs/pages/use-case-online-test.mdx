# Online Test

ikigai provides everything you need for your online tests. You can:
- **Create a test**
- **Assign tests to your students**
- **Provide feedback on student submissions**
- **Export assignment results**

To save time, ikigai offers an **AI assistant** that can generate quizzes and help you set up your online test in just 5 minutes.

## How to setup Online Test

Watch the video guides below (totaling 5 minutes) to set up your online test in ikigai.

### Feedback Your Student

Go to the submissions tab of the assignment,
then click the pencil button to start reviewing and providing feedback on student submissions.

After you provide feedback, your student will receive a notification via email. They can open the link to review your feedback.
