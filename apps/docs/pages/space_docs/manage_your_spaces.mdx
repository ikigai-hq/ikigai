# Manage your space
> This section is used for student
> In this section, we will guide you how to edit your space name and switch space

## Create new Space

To create new space, you need to click the "Space" button on the left top of page.
In the popup, click the "New Space" button to create a new space.
If created successfully, it will navigate you to the new space.

To switch spaces, click the "Space" button at the top left of the page.
In the popup, click on the desired space item in the space list to switch.
